Rum Ham

Description

One large spiral ham soaked overnight in Sailor Jerry's Spiced Rum.

Ingredients

  • 1 Dietz & Watson Tiffany Dinner Ham
  • 1 handle of Sailor Jerry Spiced Rum
  • 1L of pineaple juice
  • 100g of salt
  • 100g of brown sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add Sailor Jerry Spiced Rum to a large stockpot
  2. Bring to a boil, add brown sugar and salt, let boil for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add ham to the pot and add enough pineapple juice to cover the ham.
  4. Let simmer for 35 minutes.
  5. Remove ham and top with pineapple slices and cherry, serve.
