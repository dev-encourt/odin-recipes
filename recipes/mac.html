Mac's Famous Mac and Cheese

Description

The best mac and cheese in the suburbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Kraft Mac & Cheese
  • 1 powdered cheese packet (from the Kraft Mac & Cheese box)
  • 1.5L of water
  • 50g of butter
  • 60ml of milk
  • dog

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  3. Drain noodles, set aside.
  4. Return pot to burner over medium low heat, add butter.
  5. Add meat hunks, saute for approx. 4-5 minutes.
  6. Add milk, cheese packet, noodles, and stir.
  7. Serve immediately or store for 3-5 days.
